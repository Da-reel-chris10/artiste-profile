Biography

Christian (born Christian Alisiobi-Yobo Onyemaechi on June 13, 2005) found passion for music in his 9th grade. He claims to have been inspired by the sound track A Lot, a track by 21 Savage and J. Cole. He also listened to tracks from other musicians like Migos [ which consisted of Quavo, Offset and the late TakeOff ], DaBaby, Future and the likes, of which his favorite musician and role model appears to be Drake.

In the year 2020, Christian participated in a talent hunt show featuring all high schools in his location. He showcased his rap abilities and those abilities put him in second place under the singing category of the competition. Few months later, he opened an Audiomack account for a chance to showcase his talent to the world. His first song, Low Key, got a good number of streams, considering it was his very first track and this motivated him to keep up the good work. He then released other songs like Fallen Dreams featuring Drex Davis, Paper Cut, Olive, Demon Sounds featuring Roy and a few others.

He has also had a bunch of features from his fellow upcoming talents. Tracks he is featured in are Middle Child by Drex Davis, No Difference by Drex Davis, Past Things by Donps Carter, Grind by Alex Daniel, Sky High by Big Smoke and a few others.

Currently he has 602 followers with a sum of 3.51k plays on his Audiomack account.
